Damage-free straight hair

Melko's hair straightening treatment causes minimal damage
even when combined with digital perms, coloring, or straight perms.
Moreover, it provides an unprecedented level of smoothness and shine that lasts for an extended period.
Additionally, Melko's hair straightening treatment leaves your hair soft while preserving its natural texture.
It doesn't result in excessively straight hair, resembling typical straightening procedures.
Instead, it maintains a light, soft, and naturally straight effect over time.
