Hair salon
business

MHD, based in Okinawa, operates two domestic beauty salons under the brand name "melco Hair Design." Known for being experts in "hair straightening," with a reputation for the highest number of treatments performed worldwide, the salon started in Okinawa in 2014 and opened a private salon in Omotesando in 2022.
As their first step towards international expansion, they have established a new local subsidiary in Cambodia, with plans to open their third store, the Phnom Penh branch, around spring 2024. Following Cambodia, they intend to expand to Southeast Asian countries, Europe, Africa, and the United States, continuing their global expansion.

We believe that in considering the future development of the beauty industry, it is essential to provide value, including work styles and working environments, to the future hair professionals who will carry the industry forward. This includes training and nurturing them from the beginning, which we believe can contribute to the advancement of the industry.

Online sales
business

Melco, drawing from its extensive experience in Coconut Straightening with a record of outstanding treatment results, has developed a range of hair care products exclusively designed for Coconut Straightening. These products are formulated with an abundance of ingredients that are genuinely beneficial for hair, allowing both customers and hair professionals to experience impressive results. With the MELCOCO series, we aim to create the ideal texture—moisturized yet non-greasy, smooth yet not dry—achieving the perfect balance.
